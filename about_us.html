About Us

At Anupro360, we specialize in providing expert training and consulting services for the mining industry and other vital productive sectors. Our primary focus lies in the comprehensive training of heavy equipment operators, offering industry-recognized certifications that align with the Chilean Mining Qualification Framework and the stringent standards demanded by large-scale mining operations in Chile.

However, our extensive experience extends beyond the mining sector. We also deliver tailored consulting and training solutions to a diverse range of companies and businesses seeking to enhance their operational efficiency, implement robust safety protocols for their workforce, and optimize the overall performance of their equipment. Leveraging up-to-date methodologies and a practical, results-oriented approach, we empower our clients to achieve their critical efficiency and growth objectives.
