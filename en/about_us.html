About Us

At Anupro360, we specialize in training and consulting for the mining industry and other productive sectors. Our main focus is the training of heavy equipment operators, offering certifications aligned with the Mining Qualification Framework and the standards required in Chile's large-scale mining.

However, our experience goes beyond the mining sector. We also provide consulting and training services to companies and businesses that seek to improve their operational processes, train their staff in safety, and optimize the performance of their equipment. With updated methodologies and a practical approach, we help companies achieve their efficiency and growth objectives.
