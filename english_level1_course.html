Description of the Online Basic English Course for Iquique

Anupro360's Basic English Course (Level 1) offers you 37 lessons and 80 hours of online work, designed for you to learn at your own pace from Iquique or anywhere. Over one month, you will have access to video material, written material, and practical exercises. Our online tutor will be available to answer your questions via email or messaging within the virtual classroom. In addition, for reading and diction practice, the virtual classroom has video conference rooms.

Upon starting the course, you will take a diagnostic test to identify your English level and focus on the areas that need the most reinforcement. Get ready to communicate in English!

Online Course Delivery Method

E-learning (Asynchronous Online) with self-paced learning based on Videos and other interactive content.

Students enrolled from Iquique and other regions will access our complete virtual classroom, where they will find explanatory videos, downloadable study material, and a final evaluation to measure their progress.

The Basic English course consists of 37 lessons, totaling 80 hours of flexible online work. You can study on your own schedule during the one-month duration of the course, taking advantage of the videos, written material, and practical exercises. A tutor will be at your disposal for inquiries by email or messaging within the virtual classroom. For reading and pronunciation practice, the virtual classroom offers interactive video conference rooms.
