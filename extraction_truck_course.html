Extraction Truck (CAEX) Operator Course

This course is aimed at those who wish to learn to operate high-tonnage extraction trucks used in open-pit mining. The training covers aspects such as:

  • Fundamentals of safe and efficient operation.
  • Loading, hauling, and unloading procedures.
  • Mining safety regulations.
  • Basic equipment maintenance.
  • Factors affecting productivity and efficiency.
