Extraction Truck (CAEX) Operator Course

This comprehensive course is specifically designed for individuals aspiring to master the operation of high-tonnage extraction trucks (CAEX) commonly utilized in open-pit mining environments. The training curriculum encompasses critical aspects such as:

  • Fundamentals of safe and efficient operational practices.
  • Detailed procedures for loading, hauling, and unloading materials.
  • Comprehensive understanding of mining safety regulations and best practices.
  • Introduction to basic equipment maintenance and troubleshooting.
  • Key factors that significantly influence productivity and overall efficiency.
