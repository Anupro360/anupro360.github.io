Hydraulic Shovel Operator Course

Equipment description:

The hydraulic shovel is a fundamental piece of equipment in the mining industry, designed to perform excavation and material loading tasks efficiently. Its operation is based on a hydraulic system that allows precise control of its movements, ensuring high levels of productivity and safety in large-scale operations.

Course objective:

This course aims to train participants in the safe and efficient operation of a hydraulic shovel in mining environments. Students will acquire theoretical and practical knowledge about the operation of the equipment, safety protocols, basic maintenance, and best operating practices.

Course content:

  • Introduction to the hydraulic shovel: characteristics and functions.
  • Safety in operation and current regulations.
  • Main controls and components of the equipment.
  • Excavation and loading techniques.
  • Preventive maintenance and resolution of common problems.
  • Performance optimization in mining operations.
