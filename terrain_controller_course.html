Terrain Controller Course (Remote Extraction Truck Operator)

This course is designed for those looking to specialize in the supervision and management of autonomous extraction trucks, one of the most advanced technologies in modern mining. Through this program, students will develop key competencies to inspect, monitor, and optimize the operation of autonomous truck fleets from a control room. The contents include:

  • Remote fleet supervision and control: Real-time monitoring of operational conditions and truck performance.
  • Pre-operational inspection in autonomous operations: Verification procedures before, during, and after operation to ensure optimal performance.
  • Safety and regulations in automated mining: Risk identification and application of safety protocols in remote operation.
  • Data management and decision-making: Use of Power BI to analyze operational metrics and optimize fleet performance.

This course is aimed at operators, supervisors, and technicians seeking to adapt to new technologies and enhance their professional profile in the mining industry.
